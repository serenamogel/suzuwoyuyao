    How to Obtain the Never Lost, Always Found Emblem in Destiny 2: The Witcher Emblem Guide

    Destiny 2 players now have the chance to get the special Never Lost Always Found emblem, showcasing a silver wolf against a bold red background, by completing specific triumphs linked with The Witcher. This emblem adds a unique and themed look to Destiny 2 player collections.

    17 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Destiny 2 players now have the chance to get the special Never Lost Always Found emblem, showcasing a silver wolf against a bold red background, by completing specific triumphs linked with The Witcher. This emblem adds a unique and themed look to Destiny 2 player collections.

    Obtaining the Emblem

    To get the Never Lost Always Found emblem, players must finish the Swords and Signs triumph during Destiny 2’s Season of the Wish. This triumph requires defeating 100 enemies using swords and accomplishing 50 defeats with Solar Melee abilities. This emblem is only available during this partnership period, making it special and can’t be bought from the in-game store.

    Tips for Triumph Completion

    To complete this triumph faster, use Solar Melee abilities that recharge quickly. Abilities like Knife Trick, Throwing Hammer, or Incinerator Snap, based on your character class, work well. Boost your Strength stat and add armor mods like Melee Kickstart, Momentum Transfer, and Invigoration to shorten melee cooldowns, making the process smoother and quicker.

    Claiming Your Emblem

    Once you’ve fulfilled the requirements of the Swords and Signs triumph, head to the Triumphs menu in the game to claim your Never Lost Always Found emblem. It serves as a visual representation of your triumphs and also symbolizes the collaborative theme, marking your success uniquely.

    About Destiny 2

    Destiny 2, launched in 2017, is an online first-person shooter game by Bungie. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where Guardians, equipped with special powers and advanced weapons, defend Earth from various alien species and a looming threat known as the Darkness. It offers a diverse gaming experience by blending player versus environment (PvE) and player versus player (PvP) elements, including story missions, strikes, raids, and PvP activities.

    Gameplay and Features

    Destiny 2 combines first-person shooter dynamics with role-playing and massively multiplayer online (MMO) elements. Players use a range of weapons and powers strategically, and “Guided Games” help in efficient clan assembling for raids.

    Summary of the Exclusive Emblem

    The Never Lost Always Found emblem in Destiny 2 is a rare collectible achieved by completing triumphs in collaboration with The Witcher. Exclusive during the Season of the Wish, it showcases triumphs and thematic elements from the partnership. Using Solar Melee abilities and maximizing the Strength stat is advisable to hasten the triumph process.

    FAQs about the Never Lost Always Found Emblem in Destiny 2:

    How do I get the Never Lost Always Found emblem in Destiny 2?

    To obtain the emblem, complete the Swords and Signs triumph during the Season of the Wish by defeating 100 adversaries with swords and 50 with Solar Melee abilities.

    Any tips for faster triumph completion?

    Utilize Solar Melee abilities with quick recharge times like Knife Trick or Throwing Hammer. Also, use armor mods to decrease melee cooldowns by boosting the Strength stat.

    Where can I claim the emblem once the triumph is completed?

    After successfully finishing the Swords and Signs triumph, go to the Triumphs menu to claim your Never Lost Always Found emblem.

    Is the Never Lost Always Found emblem part of a limited-time collaboration?

    Absolutely, it’s tied to the Season of the Wish collaboration between Destiny 2 and The Witcher, making it a rare and coveted collectible.
