    Alice Braga Moraes Wiki Biography

    Alice Braga Moraes was born on the 15th April 1983 in São Paulo, Brazil, and is an actress, probably best recognized for starring in the role of Angelica in the film “City Of God”, playing Anna in the film “I Am Legend” and as Teresa Mendoza in the TV series “Queen Of The South”. Her career has been active since 1998.

    So, have you ever wondered how rich Alice Braga is, as of early 2018? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that the total size of Alice’s net worth is over $2 million, accumulated through her successful involvement in the entertainment industry as a professional actress.

    Alice Braga Net Worth $2 Million

    Alice Braga hails from a famous family, as the daughter of Ninho Moraes and Ana Maria Braga, who is a well-known actress, as well as her aunt, Sônia Braga; her sister is Rita Moraes, who is also involved in the film industry as an actress. So, under the influence of her mother and aunt, Alice became interested in acting very early on, and by the age of eight she had appeared in an advertising commercial for yogurt. Beside that, she also starred in various school plays.

    Alice’s professional acting career really began in 1998, when she made her debut appearance in the role of Claudia in a Portuguese short video “Trampolim”. Her breakthrough role came in 2002, when she appeared as Angelica in the film “City Of God”, which earned her a nomination for Best Supporting Actress at Cinema Brazil Grand Prize. Three years later, Alice featured in the role of Karinna in the film “Lower City”, winning a number of significant awards in the category for Best Actress at the Rio de Janeiro International Film Festival, ACIE Awards and São Paulo Association of Art Critics Awards, among others, all of which potentially added a considerable amount to her net worth.

    In 2006, Alice made her English-language debut appearance in the film “Journey To The End Of The Night”, after which she was cast as Anna in the 2007 film “I Am Legend”, starring along with Will Smith in the leading roles. By the end of the decade, she had also featured in the role of Julia in the film “The Milky Way” (2007), played Mireya Sanchez in the 2009 film “Crossing Over”, and appeared as Isabelle in the film “Predators” in 2010.

    At the beginning of the next decade, Alice was cast as Angeline in the horror film “The Rite” (2011), and then in Walter Salles’ film “On The Road” (2012), starring along with Kristen Stewart and Sam Riley. To speak further about her career, she portrayed Olivia in the film “Latitudes”, directed by Felipe Braga, and Vania in the film “Ardor”, both in 2014. Most recently, she was chosen to play Teresa Mendoza in the TV series “Queen Of The South” (2016-2017), and starred as Sophia in the 2017 film “The Shack”. It is also announced that she will appear in the role of Dr. Cecilia Reyes in the film entitled “The New Mutants”, which will be released in 2019, so her net worth will certainly increase in the forthcoming years.

    When it comes to Alice Braga’s personal life, there is no information about it in the media, except that she was in a relationship with Mexican actor Diego Luna from 2004 to 2005, so she is believed to still be single.
