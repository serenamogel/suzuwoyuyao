    Caramel Apple Shake

    This refreshing Caramel Apple Shake is made with apple cider, vanilla ice cream and caramel!

    Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword caramel apple shake Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 2 Calories 283kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 oz apple cider NOT apple juice
    • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
    • 1 tbsp caramel topping 1 to 2 tbsp
    • whipped cream optional

    Instructions

    • Blend apple cider, vanilla ice cream and caramel topping together.

    • Serve with whipped cream on top. Drizzle with caramel if desired.
