    Miro shares a positive update about CJ Perry.

    AEW star CJ Perry was scheduled to accompany Andrade El Idolo to the ring at the CMLL event on December 15. She was absent from the show, and she also missed the AEW Collision taping on Saturday. She took to social media and noted that she had to go to the emergency room due to an infection that went up her arm.

    On December 18, Perry posted that she would be undergoing surgery, as the infection had gotten worse.

    In a post on Instagram stories, Miro shared a picture of Perry in her hospital bed. He wrote that she was out of surgery, so it was time to eat.

    WrestleZone wishes CJ Perry well in her recovery, and we will provide more information as it becomes available.
