    Matthew Lillard’s age

    Matthew Lillard was born on January 24, 1970, in Lansing, Michigan.

    He is currently 53 years old.

    He later studied at Fullerton College, the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, and the Circle in the Square Theatre School.
