    Robert Boyd Holbrook (born September 1, 1981) is an actor from the United States. He has appeared as DEA agent Steve Murphy in the Netflix series Narcos (2015-2017), as Corinthian in The Sandman (2022-), and as “Cap” Hatfield in the History miniseries Hatfields & McCoys.

    Who is Boyd Holbrook wife Tatiana Pajkovic?

    Tatiana Pajkovic, Holbrook’s wife, is a Danish actress and singer who rose to prominence following her marriage to Holbrook. She has only four credits in her acting career: Nynne, Det vildeste westen, The Transporter Refuelled, and Devil Town.

    Holbrook was discovered while working as a carpenter for Jenny Wiley Theatre in Kentucky on the side. In 2001, he signed with Elite Models. He has previously worked for agencies such as Wilhelmina Models. Holbrook has modeled for Gucci, Jean Paul Gaultier, Versace, Hugo Boss, Bill Blass, Calvin Klein, Moschino, Marc Jacobs, and DSquared2.

    Holbrook and model Omahyra Mota were the subjects of photographer Ellen Von Unwerth’s Paris gallery exhibition “Omahyra & Boyd,” as well as Exhibitions International’s 2005 book of the same name (ISBN 2-914171-20-X).
