What are evaluation Standards?

04 Aug 2024, 00:00

Evaluation standards identify how the quality of an evaluation will be judged. They can be used when planning an evaluation as well as for meta-evaluation (evaluating the evaluation). Many organizations have guidelines which address issues of quality and ethics together. What are the four standards of evaluation? Feasibility standards ensure that an evaluation will be realistic, prudent, diplomatic and frugal. Propriety standards ensure that an evaluation will be conducted legally, ethically and with due regard for the welfare of those involved in the evaluation, as well as those affected by its results.
