    How do I permanently delete my Brainly account?

    The option to delete your account can be found in your Profile Settings under Privacy. Click on the box labeled I want to delete my account, and the request will be sent for the account to be deleted.

    How can I delete my account?

    Open up the mobile app on your iPhone or Android Device. Log in and go to App Settings to find the Delete Account button at the bottom. Tap it then confirm. The data and matches are wiped.
