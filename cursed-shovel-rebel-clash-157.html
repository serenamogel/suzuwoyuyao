    Cursed Shovel (Japanese: のろいのスコップ Curse Scoop) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the Rebel Clash expansion.

    Card text

    Attach a Pokémon Tool to 1 of your Pokémon that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached.
    If the Pokémon this card is attached to is Knocked Out by damage from an opponent's attack, discard the top 2 cards of your opponent's deck.
    You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.

    Trivia

    Origin

    This card depicts the shovel on top of Palossand. As such, the Sand Sink attack on the Palossand released during the same expansion interacts with it.
