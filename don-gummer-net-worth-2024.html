    Don Gummer was born in Louisville, Kentucky, and raised in Indiana. He attended Ben Davis High School in Indianapolis and later enrolled at the Herron School of Art, also in Indianapolis.     Gummer’s artistic career began in the late 1970s, with his first solo show held in 1973. His work primarily consists of tabletop and wall-mounted sculptures, as well as larger freestanding works, often in bronze. His art is characterized by its abstract nature, with a strong emphasis on geometry and balance.
