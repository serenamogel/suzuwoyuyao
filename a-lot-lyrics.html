    “Alot” is a song by American rapper 21 Savage featuring J. Cole.

    It is the lead single from Savage’s second studio album, I Am Greater Than I Was (2018).

    And it was released on January 11, 2019.

    A Lot Lyrics was penned by

    • 21 Savage
    • J. Cole
    • Pierre Bourne
    • Metro Boomin
    • Buddah Bless and DJ Mustard

    Overall, “Alot” was a successful song due to a combination of factors, including 21 Savage’s popularity.

    The song was a commercial success, reaching number 12 on the US Billboard and Grammy Awards for Best Rap Performance in 2019.
