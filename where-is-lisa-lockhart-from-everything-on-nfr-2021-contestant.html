    Lisa is no rookie to the Rodeo world. She is a 14-time WNFR qualifier, 2 time Reserve World Champion, 2-time NFR Average Champion, ERA Champion, and a four-time Canadian Champion.

    In addition to that, Lisa has won the Worlds biggest one-day rodeo held in the Dallas Cowboy Stadium “The American”, twice.

    Further, Lisa also won the Average at the National Finals Rodeo in Las Vegas, Nevada in December 2014 and 2016.

    Where Is Lisa Lockhart From?
